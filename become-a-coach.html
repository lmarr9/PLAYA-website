Eligibility

Who can coach on Playa?

Playa is open to any currently enrolled college athlete in good standing with their program. Here's what we verify:

Active enrollment

You must be currently enrolled at a 2- or 4-year college or university.

Active athlete status

You must be on an active roster at an NCAA, NAIA, or JUCO program.

NIL compliance

Your coaching activity must comply with your school's NIL policy and applicable NCAA/NAIA rules.

Identity verification

We verify your identity and student-athlete status before your profile goes live.

Not sure if you qualify? Email us at athletes@playaapp.com and we'll walk you through it.
