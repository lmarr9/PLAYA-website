Players pay a small service fee at checkout on top of the coach's listed rate. This fee covers platform operations, payment processing, and safety features. The exact amount is shown transparently before you confirm any booking.
Coaches are paid via direct deposit within 48 hours of each completed session. Payments are processed through Stripe. Coaches link their bank account during onboarding and receive 100% of their listed rate — no deductions.
