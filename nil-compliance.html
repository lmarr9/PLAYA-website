Section 01

Overview

Playa is built with NIL (Name, Image, and Likeness) compliance at its core. We are committed to helping college athletes monetize their skills legally and transparently under current NCAA and institutional NIL rules.

Section 02

What Is NIL?

NIL refers to a college athlete's right to profit from their Name, Image, and Likeness. Since the NCAA's landmark 2021 ruling, college athletes across all divisions can earn money through activities like coaching, appearances, and endorsements without losing eligibility.

Section 03

How Playa Supports NIL

Playa operates as a marketplace where college athletes offer coaching services — a permitted NIL activity. Athletes set their own rates, manage their own schedules, and keep 100% of their earnings. Playa does not take a cut from coach earnings.

Section 04

Coach Responsibilities

Coaches are responsible for understanding and complying with their individual school's NIL policies and any applicable state laws. Some institutions require athletes to disclose NIL activities or register with a compliance office. Playa encourages all coaches to consult with their athletic department before joining.

Section 05

Disclosure Requirements

Many schools and states require athletes to disclose NIL deals. Playa provides coaches with a summary of their earnings activity upon request to assist with any required institutional disclosures.

Section 06

Eligibility Safeguards

Playa verifies that all coaches are currently enrolled college athletes. We do not allow compensation structures that could jeopardize eligibility. Playa does not offer coaches any benefits contingent on athletic performance or recruitment.

Section 07

State and Institutional Variation

NIL rules vary by state and by institution. While federal guidance provides a baseline, individual schools may have stricter requirements. It is the coach's responsibility to comply with all applicable rules at their institution.

Section 08

Updates to NIL Rules

The NIL landscape continues to evolve. Playa monitors regulatory changes and updates its practices accordingly. Check this page periodically for updates. For questions contact compliance@playaapp.com.
