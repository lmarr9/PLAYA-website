Safety & Trust

Every athlete verified. Every session protected.

Safety isn't an afterthought on Playa — it's baked into every step of the experience, from profile creation to post-session review.

Verification

How we verify every coach

Before any coach can appear in search results, they pass a multi-step verification process. No exceptions.

1

Identity Verification

Every coach submits a government-issued ID and their school-issued student email. We cross-reference both against enrollment records to confirm identity and active student status.

2

Athlete Status Confirmation

Coaches provide proof of active roster membership — official team rosters, athletic department confirmation, or a coach verification letter. We confirm they are a currently competing college athlete at an NCAA, NAIA, or JUCO program.

3

NIL & Compliance Review

We verify that the athlete's coaching activity is compliant with their school's NIL policy and applicable governing body rules before approving their profile. Our compliance team reviews each application manually.

4

Profile Approval

Only after all three steps clear does a coach profile go live on the platform. Verified profiles display a green checkmark badge so players always know who's been cleared.

Safety Features

Built-in protections for everyone

Playa's safety features protect both players and coaches throughout every interaction on the platform.

Verified Badge System

Every coach who passes verification receives a visible green checkmark badge on their profile. Players always know they're booking a real, verified college athlete — not an impostor.

In-App Messaging Only

All communication between players and coaches stays within the Playa app. We prohibit sharing personal contact information before booking, protecting everyone's privacy.

Secure Payments

All payments are processed through Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor. No cash, no Venmo — every transaction is tracked and secured within the app.

Two-Way Rating System

After every session, both players and coaches leave ratings and reviews. This public accountability system helps surface great coaches and flags bad actors early.

One-Tap Reporting

Any user can report a concern from within the app in one tap. Our trust and safety team reviews every report within 24 hours. Serious violations result in immediate suspension.

Minor Safeguards

When a player indicates they are under 18, additional safeguards apply — including parental consent requirements and restrictions on session location types.
