Our Mission

Our mission

Playa exists to make elite athletic coaching accessible to everyone. We connect players of all ages and skill levels with verified college athletes for real, in-person training sessions. No barriers. No gatekeeping. Just great coaching.

Our Story

How it started

Playa was founded after watching too many young athletes struggle to find quality coaching that fit their schedule and budget. Club fees were high, private trainers were expensive, and the best athletes in any community — college players — had no way to share their skills and earn income doing it. Playa changes that equation for both sides.
